Customer experience in health insurance is about keeping members healthy and happy. However, inadequate engagement with members in realizing their health goals and a one-size fits-all approach from payers towards engagement continue to remain big challenges in achieving this goal. With rising consumerism, cost of care and technology adoption, payers are increasingly looking to build a comprehensive understanding of their members to drive engagement in achieving targeted and superior outcomes.

This webinar will demonstrate how payers are leveraging personalization techniques in driving engagement with members. Using a member journey of ‘Enabling long term health and wellness’, we will present options available with a payer to uniquely engage and encourage a member in participating in his wellbeing

What will it cover?

  • How payers can leverage personalization to drive superior customer experience in healthcare
  • Role of technology like AI and ML in enhancing engagement
  • Options to engage and encourage members to participate in their wellbeing
  • Leveraging technology to understand payers in order to drive superior outcomes
  • Demonstration of our AI driven personalized member engagement solution

Why attend the webinar?

  • Healthcare Payer solutions that fail to engage members, increase cost by 21%
  • 73% members are likely to recommend brands with good engagement programs
  • If engaged, 96% consumers would be healthier, reducing care costs drastically
  • Consumers directly control $330 billion annually in out-of-pocket healthcare expenses
